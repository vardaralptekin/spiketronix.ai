Why edge intelligence must change

Cloud transfer costs too much. Idle waiting burns the rest.

Local by design

The intelligence has to live on the device: compute locally, stay asleep until an event, and send only the decision.

Power

Years on a coin cell, or self-powered.

Speed

Local inference removes network delay and reacts while the signal still matters.

Safety

Catch the fault before the machine fails.

Continuous local sensing keeps critical data private and surfaces early warning patterns.

Non-volatile FeRAM

Remembers the model with power removed.

GF 22FDX FeRAM retains every weight with the chip fully off. Instant-on state removes the standby cost of remembering the model.

Weights stay in place Read + compute
In-memory compute

Computes where the weights are stored.

Read and compute become one step. No memory bus, no repeated fetch, and no energy spent moving weights to a separate compute unit.
